## Slide 2 — Product Overview

**PaceWell**

**Value proposition:** Understand your heart rate, spot your triggers, and pace your day with confidence.

**Pain point:** POTS and dysautonomia patients get sudden tachycardia from normal daily life. Crashes feel random. Trackers show numbers — not why their heart raced or what to do next.

**Solution:** Web app syncing Fitbit HR, logging activities/symptoms, detecting spikes, Gemini AI analysis, clinician reports. Powered by Butterbase. Live on Netlify + Render.
