Slide 2 · Product Overview

PaceWell

Understand your heart rate. Prevent crashes.
Pace your day with confidence.

User pain point
People with POTS and dysautonomia get sudden tachycardia from normal daily activities. Crashes feel random. They can't explain weeks of symptoms to their doctor. Fitness trackers show numbers — not why their heart raced or what to do next.
Our built solution
PaceWell syncs wearable HR data, lets users log activities and symptoms, automatically detects spike events, and uses Gemini AI to surface triggers and pacing guidance — plus clinician-ready reports. Powered by Butterbase backend + deployed on Netlify & Render.
HR timeline + spike detection Activity & symptom logging Analyze My Day (Gemini AI) Clinician reports Fitbit / Google Health sync Realistic POTS demo data Butterbase waitlist API
